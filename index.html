About Us

International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) was Established as a global non-governmental and non-profit organization, IAESTE is dedicated to facilitating mutually beneficial partnerships between students, employers, and universities through its esteemed internship program.

History of IAESTE

In 1948, James Newby initiated a youth exchange program promoting global student experiences for future leaders' cross-cultural competence.

Global Oppurtunities in IAESTE

Operating in 80+ countries, IAESTE provides a gateway to diverse international experiences. Join our global community for a journey that expands your horizons both professionally and personally.

Our Mission

IAESTE is committed to fostering international collaboration and professional development by providing students with valuable opportunities to gain practical experience and cross-cultural skills through paid internships abroad.
